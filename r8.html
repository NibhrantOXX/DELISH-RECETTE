Mawa Bati

With the right amount of sweetness, perfect crisp and texture, MawaBati is one the best sweet palates of Madhya Pradesh. The preparation includes Mava dough mixed with a generous amount of dry fruits. After frying the Mava balls, it is dipped in sugar syrup to add to the sweetness of the dish. This is one dish you cannot miss out when in Madhya Pradesh.

To prepare this famous dish,here's the recipe

INGREDIENTS REQUIRED:

  • 200 g Khoya
  • 400 g Maida
  • 350 ml Oil
  • 200 g Sugar
  • 300 ml Water
  • 6 Kaju
  • 6 Almond
  • 1/2 tsp Cardamom powder
  • Pinch Salt

Here are the steps that you need to follow--->

  1. Knead a dough with maida, pinch of salt and 100 ml water in a medium sized bowl. After that divide the dough into small-sized balls.v
  2. Take the already prepared khoa and mix chopped dry fruits into it.
  3. Take 1 ball, make a small hole with index finger then keep on spreading until it gains a bowl shape. Then fill the khoya and close it by twisting and pressing it between your palms.
  4. Heat the oil in a kadhai and side by prepare 1 string sugar syrup. Gently lower the mawa bati in hot oil. Cook it until it becomes golden brown in low to medium flame.
  5. After frying them put into sugar syrup and immediately transfer them in plate.
  6. Tempting MAWA BATI is ready to be served.
